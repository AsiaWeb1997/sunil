Why Is My Baby Genius?

Window of Opportunity/ Neuron Connectivity

Baby is born with 100 billion neurons (brain cells). At early age, connections between neurons are made very rapidly - at least 1 million neuron connections every second - far more than any other time in life. How OurGenius Baby Program is different?

Our Genius Baby Program
Interaction and program personalization

Regular interactions to support the program being tailor made just towards enhancing your baby's development and requirements. Based on the interactions, feedback and attachments/aversions of your baby, the course content is continuously monitored, geared, and changed. This ensures maximum enhancement of the skills through development of your baby's interest.

Wholesome Program

The only program that covers all areas of parenting and baby development (brain development, physical development, nutritional balance and other guidance).

One size fits all?
