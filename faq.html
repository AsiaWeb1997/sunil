Frequently Asked Questions

To force a child between age of 5 to 7 years (or for any age when they are not interested in the subject which results in forcing) for reading/ understanding numbers is indeed a pressure. However, providing exposure and satisfying baby's curiosity when they actually enjoy it most is enhancing and embracing their window of opportunity. Parents have increasingly noticed that babies enjoy the experience thoroughly and as a result do not stress on subjects and easily master them when they grow up.It is our belief that teaching children to read/ understand concepts at a young enough age, when they are naturally willing to learn, frees them from the potential burden of learning the same in school. We believe that it is learning to read too late that actually causes the process to become burdensome. You may know this from specific expereinces shared by parents.
